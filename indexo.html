Digital Educational Platform

The purpose of the Center is to provide modern digital services within the scientific and educational platform of ETU "LETI"

D.E.P tasks:

  • Increase the efficiency of service delivery
  • Personalize approaches to the learner
  • Increase the availability and attractiveness of scientific activities

Internal Participants Of Scientific And Educational Activities

Student & Staff Portal

Access is restricted and only students and staff which registered in the system are allowed to view their registered modules and acces to private email accounts and student details.
